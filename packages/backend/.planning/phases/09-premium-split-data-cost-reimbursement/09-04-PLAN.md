## totalDataSpend definition (RESEARCH Open Question 2 recommendation, L338-341 / L318)
`totalDataSpend` = the protocol's ACTUAL data outlay = the $199 capital (ONCE per epoch) + Σ per-position
hedge fills. Per-premium φ_data slices ROUTED into the vault are *recoupment* of the one $199, NOT new
spend — so the vault-side accumulator counts the $199 capital ONCE (not on each routing). The two clean
assertions (RESEARCH L341): (a) `Σφ_data(vault) >= DATA_CAPITAL_USD` (recoupment break-even) AND (b)
`DATA_CAPITAL_USD + Σhedge == totalDataSpend` (disjointness — the $199 counted once, the hedge ledger N×).
Assertion (b) is the load-bearing `invariant_dataCostConserved`; (a) may be a supporting assertion.
