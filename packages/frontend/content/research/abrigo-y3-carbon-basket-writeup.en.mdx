The Y₃ × carbon basket iteration investigates whether user-side trading volume of the carbon asset basket on Celo (Mento stablecoins + global assets) is a reliable proxy for capital flow in frontier markets. The basket comprises ten verified Celo assets: COPM, USDm, EURm, BRLm, KESm, XOFm, native CELO, USDT (0x48065fbb…), USDC, and WETH (Celo native bridge).

The panel spans 82 Fridays from 2024-08-30 to 2026-04-03, built from `carboncontroller_evt_tokenstraded` events of the Carbon DeFi Celo protocol. The user filter excludes Arb Fast Lane traffic (`0x8c05ea…`). The basket's user-only aggregate volume showed 77 non-zero weeks out of 82 possible observations (n_nonzero = 77).

Calibration reached a structural-HALT disposition: n_nonzero (77) falls below the pre-committed N_MIN (80) by three weeks. Per protocol design §4, the model does not silently pivot to arbitrageur volume. A pathological-HALT disposition memo was filed. The next stage requires a user decision on pivot strategy: extend the panel window, replace the basket, or proceed with an adjusted robustness threshold.
