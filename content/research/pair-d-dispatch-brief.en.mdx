The lag pattern β_6/β_9/β_12 is positive at all horizons, with β_6 capturing approximately 80% of the composite effect (lag-6 dominance, RC FLAG #3). All four robustness checks R1–R4 agree in sign; none triggers SUBSTRATE_TOO_NOISY. The joint step 4(a) §8.1 test returns PASS, unblocking Stage-2.

The empirical claim carried into the M-sketch: a 1% peso devaluation today is associated with a measurable rise in young-worker services share, concentrated at the 6-month horizon within — but not uniformly across — the 6–12-month BPO-contracting window. The relationship is statistically extremely strong; the correlation is identified but the causal channel is not (RC FLAG #1).
