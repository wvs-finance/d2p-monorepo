## KaTeX Math Pipeline Test

This is the English-locale spike fixture for verifying the build-time KaTeX math pipeline.

Let $\hat{\beta} = +0.137$ be the OLS estimator for the swap-spread sensitivity coefficient
of the Abrigo Y3 hedge instrument.

The expected payoff function of the instrument is approximated as:

$$
\Pi(\sigma_T) \approx \hat{K} \cdot \sigma_T \tag{1}
$$

where $\sigma_T$ is the terminal volatility and $\hat{K} > 0$ is the estimated scale factor.
Equation (1) is the centerpiece of the CFMM analytical framework for Pair D.

:::theorem{label="Estimator Convergence"}
Under standard Gauss-Markov regularity conditions, the estimator $\hat{\beta}$
converges in probability to the population parameter $\beta$ as $n \to \infty$.
This validates the use of $\hat{\beta}$ as a proxy for the structural coefficient in the
spread-sensitivity analysis.
:::

The above result confirms that Equation (1) has asymptotic support under standard assumptions.
