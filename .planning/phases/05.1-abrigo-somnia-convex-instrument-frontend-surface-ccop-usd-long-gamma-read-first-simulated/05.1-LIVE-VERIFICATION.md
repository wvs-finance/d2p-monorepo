### Issues found

1. **MINOR — schematic provenance tier not rendered as a pill (Claim 2).** Only 2 of the 3 advertised provenance tiers (fork-fixture, spec) render as icon+text+aria `ProvenancePill`s. The "schematic" tier — with authored `provenance.schematic` ("Esquemático"/"Schematic") and `schematic_aria` i18n keys present in `messages/{es-CO,en}/instruments.json` — is never mounted in the DOM. The chart's "(esquemático)" inline label substitutes for it, but it is not an aria-labelled, icon-bearing pill, so the "three tiers visually + textually distinct as pills" portion of the claim is overstated. Not a fishing/honesty regression (it does not present fake data), so non-blocking, but the claim wording should be corrected or a schematic pill added next to the payoff diagram.

2. **MINOR — Recharts ResponsiveContainer `width(-1)/height(-1)` warning on mount.** A console *warning* (not error) fires before the container measures; visible symptom is the curve missing from a full-page screenshot taken during the entrance animation. Curve paints correctly once measured (element screenshot confirms). No hydration/error impact. Consider an explicit min-height/aspect on the chart container to silence it and harden screenshot-based regression tests.

3. **INFO (not a route bug) — 2 console errors are external WalletConnect/Reown calls** (`pulse.walletconnect.org` 400, `api.web3modal.org/appkit/v1/config` 403) due to the `placeholder_walletconnect_id_for_dev` projectId in local dev. These are third-party network failures, NOT app/React errors, and do not affect any honesty claim. Will not occur with a real projectId.

### Gate verdict

**PASS WITH ONE PARTIAL** — Claims 1, 3, 4, 5 are ✓ PASS; Claim 2 is ⚠ PARTIAL (2/3 tiers render as pills; schematic tier label present but not a pill). No honesty/anti-fishing regression observed: no fork-fixture or spec value is presented as live/market data, the wallet never implies a live connection, no fabricated numbers, and the instrument JSON-LD omits strike/slope/address while asserting simulated:true. The Claim 2 gap is cosmetic/semantic, not a fishing risk — recommend recording a waiver or adding the schematic pill before the wording in the plan is treated as fully satisfied.
